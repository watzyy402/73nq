Methodology

STDV & OTE.

OTE
Optimal Trade Entry
0.62 — 0.79 Fibonacci Retracement

The OTE is the institutional entry window — the zone where algorithms re-engage after sweeping liquidity. When price delivers into 0.62–0.79 of a prior swing following a clean displacement, smart money is building positions. Every entry I take is anchored here. Wait for the algorithm to come to you.

STDV
Standard Deviation
Algorithmic Price Delivery Levels

STDV models how far price is programmed to travel from an anchor. Using ±1, ±2, ±2.5 standard deviation extensions, we project exact delivery targets before price gets there. This is not probability — it's the algorithm's math made visible.

Confluence — When they meet, you act

The real edge is the intersection. When price retraces into the OTE zone (0.62–0.79) and simultaneously arrives at a ±1 or ±2 STDV extension, the algorithm is printing its footprint. This is what separates structured entries from random ones. Both must align. If they don't — there is no trade.
