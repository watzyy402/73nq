Methodology

STDV & OTE.

OTE
Optimal Trade Entry
0.62 — 0.79 Fibonacci Retracement

The OTE is the institutional entry window — the precise zone where algorithms re-engage after a liquidity sweep. When price delivers into 0.62–0.79 of a prior swing following a clean displacement, you are in the zone where smart money builds positions. Every entry I take is anchored here. No guessing, no chasing. Wait for the algorithm to come to you.

STDV
Standard Deviation
Algorithmic Price Delivery Levels

STDV models how far price is algorithmically programmed to travel from an anchor point. Using ±1, ±2, ±2.5 standard deviation extensions, we project exact delivery targets before price gets there. This is not probability — this is the algorithm's math made visible. When STDV extension aligns with OTE, the trade becomes inevitable.

∩Confluence — When they meet, you act

The real edge is the intersection. When price retraces into the OTE zone (0.62–0.79) and simultaneously delivers into a ±1 or ±2 STDV extension, you have an institutional signature — the algorithm is printing its footprint on the chart. This is what separates structured entries from random ones. Both must align. If they don't, there is no trade.
